Got Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your mobile detail.

What areas do you service?

We're a fully mobile detailing service and come directly to your home or office. We service Johannesburg and surrounding areas — if you're unsure, just contact us and we'll confirm availability.

How long does a detail take?

It depends on the service and the condition of the vehicle. On average: Wash & Wax 1.5-2 hours, Interior Valet 2-3 hours, Paint Enhancement 3-4 hours, and 2 Step Paint Correction 5-7 hours.

Can you remove scratches or stains?

We can significantly improve light scratches, swirl marks, and stains. For deeper defects, we'll advise you on the best solution during an on-site inspection.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Card, EFT, and Cash. Payment is due upon completion of the service unless otherwise arranged.
